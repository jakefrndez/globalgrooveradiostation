Promotional Plan

Global Groove Radio Social Media Campaign

Schedule of posting: 1st week of Quarter 3

Target Audience: Teenagers

Social Media Posts

TIKTOK • Create short, engaging videos showcasing the station's music and personality. Use popular trends and challenges to attract younger listeners.

FACEBOOK • Share the posters around the area we want to target, accompanied by music from that region. Use captions that encourage listeners to tune in and discover new sounds.

PROMOTIONAL VIDEO • This promotional video for Global Groove Radio will be 1-2 minutes long and feature upbeat, global music. It will show fast-paced montage of people enjoying music from various countries.Animated graphics will showcase the station's features and benefits, while the narration will highlight its unique offerings. The video will also include testimonials from listeners, short clips of popular songs from different countries, and text overlays emphasizing key messages. The tone will be fun, energetic, and engaging, capturing the spirit of global music and community. For TikTok, the video will focus on short, trendy clips with popular challenges and dances, while Facebook will include longer segments showcasing the station's features and benefits. The video will end with social media handles and the website URL.
