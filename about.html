Flow of our stream

It begins with the preparation phase, which involves researching global music trends in the country chosen and preparing a playlist of global music from that country. This is followed by writing down talking points for the discussions that will take place during the stream, and thinking about ways to interact with listeners.

The main body of the flow chart shows the three events that will be covered in the stream: Global Groove Mix, Global Groove Talk, and Global Groove Chillout.

Global Groove Mix is a mix of global music from all over the world, with a focus on genres to the country chosen by the watcher. The discussion during this event will focus on the diversity of global music.

Global Groove Talk is an upbeat and danceable music event that will feature conversations between listeners and guests on the topic of global music. The discussion will also include personal experiences and stories related to the music.

Global Groove Chillout is a relaxing music event that will feature calming and atmospheric music. The discussion during this event will focus on relaxation, and listeners will have the opportunity to ask questions and interact with each other on the stream.
